evolution of rulership

what holds sovereignty (in the eye of those governed):
- the crown (or any holdable object):
  whoever holds the crown is the sovereign
- the throne (or any non-movable object or place):
  whoever sits on the throne is the sovereign
- the bloodline (or any other form of family tie):
  whoever inherits the blood (plus other rules) is the sovereign regardless if the have the throne or crown

sovereign over what (in the eye of the ruler):
- the land:
  the ruler governs over anyone that lives on a particular territory
- the people:
  the ruler governs over any member of the ethnic group no matter where they lives


  split sovereignty:
  a polity where the one that holds the crown rules over the people and the one how sits in the throne governs over the land
